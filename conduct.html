Code of Conduct

FUBK Flow Open-Source Project

Our Commitment

Welcome to the FUBK Flow project! We are a community of students from all levels (100L to 400L) at the Federal University Birnin Kebbi, working together to build something valuable for our campus.

This Code of Conduct is our shared promise to each other. We are dedicated to maintaining a professional, welcoming, and collaborative environment where every member can learn, contribute, and grow. We commit to ensuring a safe and inclusive space for everyone, regardless of experience level, department, gender, or personal background.

Our Community Standards

We expect all members of our project community to help us create a positive environment. Examples of behavior we encourage include:

  • Being Respectful and Professional: Treat all members with the courtesy and professionalism expected in a university setting.
  • Embracing Collaboration: This project is a mix of all levels. We encourage 400L students to mentor and 100L students to ask questions. Be patient, be helpful, and be open to learning from everyone.
  • Giving and Receiving Feedback: Offer constructive feedback focused on the project, and accept feedback with an open mind. We are all here to learn.
  • Using Welcoming Language: Be inclusive and considerate in your communication, both online and in person.
  • Staying Focused: Keep discussions and contributions relevant to the project goals to make everyone's time productive.

Behavior that is not acceptable in our community includes:

  • Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind.
  • Demeaning, offensive, or derogatory comments. This includes trolling, personal attacks, or any form of bullying.
  • Unwelcome sexual attention or the use of sexualized language and imagery.
  • Publishing someone's private information (e.g., phone number, home address) without their permission.
  • Any other conduct that would be considered unprofessional or inappropriate in a university or workplace.

Where This Applies

This Code of Conduct applies to all official FUBK Flow project channels. This includes our GitHub repository (issues, pull requests), our official WhatsApp or Telegram groups, and any on-campus meetings or project-related events.

Reporting and Enforcement

The project maintainers (team leads and senior contributors) are committed to fairly and consistently enforcing this Code.

Your safety and comfort are our priority. If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code, or have any other concerns, please contact the project team immediately at fubkflow@gmail.com.

All reports will be reviewed, investigated, and treated with strict confidentiality. We will take whatever action we deem necessary and appropriate to resolve the issue, from a private warning to removal from the project.

Our Shared Goal

This project is an opportunity to build our skills, help our fellow students, and represent our departments and university well. By participating, you agree to help us build a positive and professional community.

Let's build something great, together.
