Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12-11-2025

Welcome to the FUBK Launchpad. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your personal information for the FUBK Flow open-source project.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide through the contributor application form:

  • Identification Data: Your Full Name, Official Email Address, and Admission Number.
  • Profile Data: Your Current Level and Department.
  • Contribution Data: Your stated Programming Experience, Preferred Roles, Weekly Commitment, and answers to the Skill Challenge and motivation questions.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used *exclusively* for the following purposes:

  • To verify your status as a student at FUBK.
  • To assess your application and skill level for contribution.
  • To contact you regarding your application and to onboard you to the project (e.g., send you a GitHub repository or WhatsApp group invite).
  • To manage project roles (e.g., assigning "ClassRep" status via PeerVerify).

3. Data Storage and Security

Your application data is stored securely in a Supabase (PostgreSQL) database. Access is strictly limited to the project's core administrators. We do not (and will not) share, sell, or rent your personal information with any third-party services or advertisers.

The main FUBK Flow mobile app uses Firebase for its backend. Your user profile in that app (email, level, department) is stored securely in Firestore and is governed by these same privacy principles.

4. Your Rights

As this is a student-run, open-source project, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data you submitted.
  • Request the deletion of your application data at any time.

To do so, please contact the project maintainers at fubkflow@gmail.com.
